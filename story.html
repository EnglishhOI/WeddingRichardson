Our story

Told by:

It all started on the 6th February 2019 when Carla was invited to a server I was talking in by one of our closest friends named Alejandro Ong Baldos, also referred to as “Al”. After some time talking in the server, we decided to take our conversation into DMs where we spent the next few hours getting to know each other. 12 days of talking passed and I knew that I liked Carla, her looks, her personality, and her attitude towards life. And on day 12, which was the 18th February I asked Carla to be my girlfriend. It was a great sound to hear Carla mumble the words “yes”, after that we were a bit shy because we didn’t know what to do but after some time the awkward silence that lingered the air once again became filled with endless chatter.

Months later, Me and Carla were a lot more comfortable around each other and we would stay up playing games, watching anime, and we started to talk more deeply about what our goals and ambitions were in life, It was around this time where I became much more interested in Carla and saw a future that had her in it, people were confused at first when I told them the story but they don’t understand the importance of mental attraction in someone.
